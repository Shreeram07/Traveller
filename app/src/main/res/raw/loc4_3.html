·       In Indonesia, the traffic moves on the left hand side and cars overtake on the right.

·       It is recommended that you do not drive in Indonesia if you have never driven in Asian traffic before. The rules and regulations differ from other countries and in many places they are not taken seriously.

·       Many of the streets in Indonesia are quite narrow If you are a foreigner, you are allowed to use an International Driver’s License to drive in Indonesia.

·       Since driving can become a nightmare in Indonesia, hiring a driver is the best idea.
