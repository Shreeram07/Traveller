Quick Facts

·      PASSPORT VALIDITY:

Must be valid on the date of entry

·      BLANK PASSPORT PAGES:

One page required for entry stamp

·      TOURIST VISA REQUIRED:

Yes

·      VACCINATIONS:

None 

·      CURRENCY RESTRICTIONS FOR ENTRY:

None; amounts in excess of 10,000 BR must be declared to Customs.

·      CURRENCY RESTRICTIONS FOR EXIT:

None; amounts in excess of 10,000 BR must be declared to Customs.

Visas & Fees for Brazil. Brazil is one of the more expensive countries in South America to travel or live in, and it charges upon entry as well. To visit the country U.S. citizens will need to pay $160 USD, Canadians $65 USD, and Australians $35 USD.
