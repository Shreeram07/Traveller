Climate

Average temperature

Sydney: Annual Weather Averages. January is the hottest month in Sydney with an average temperature of 23°C (73°F) and the coldest is July at 13°C (55°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 9 in December. The wettest month is March with an average of 164mm of rain.

 

Best time to visit

The best times to visit Sydney are September through November and from March to May. These months skirt Sydney's high and low seasons, offering visitors comfortable temperatures and manageable tourist crowds.

 

High season

Prices fall during these shoulder seasons, making it more affordable to visit in the spring and fall. Thanks to Australia's location in the Southern Hemisphere, Sydney's warmest weather and peak tourist season falls between December and February (America's winter).

 

Low season

The low season in Australia, from October to March, might be a little less crowded, but it's generally one of the worst times of year to visit. Much of the country, including the Red Center and much of Western Australia, is simply too hot and humid for comfortable travel. Many hotels and attractions also close during this time, and floods are also a serious problem in much of the country. However, if you visit at this time, many hotels offer discounts.
