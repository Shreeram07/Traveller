VISA

The Consulate General charges application fees and the standards are,

(1) for regular Australian passport holders, AU$60 (single-entry visa), AU$90 (double-entry visa), AU$120 (multi-entry within 6 months), and AU$180 (multi-entry within 12 months).

(2) for the US regular passport holders, AU$169 (all visa types).

(3) for Serbian regular passport holders, AU$70 (all visa types).

(4) for Romanian regular passport holders, AU$85 (single-entry visa), AU$110 (double-entry visa), and AU$165 (multi-entry visa).

(5) for passport holders of other countries, AU$50 (single-entry visa), AU$75 (double-entry visa), AU$100 (multi-entry within 6 months), and AU$150 (multi-entry within 12 months).

(6) Normally, it takes 4 working days to process a visa. For example, if you lodge your application on Monday, you may collect it on Thursday. Express services are available under some conditions. Urgent application processing takes 3 working days and AU$30 extra per visa. Emergency application processing takes 2 working days and AU$50 extra per visa.
