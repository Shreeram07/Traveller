Average temperature

The uniformly warm waters that make up 81% of Indonesia's area ensure that temperatures on land remain fairly constant, with the coastal plains averaging 28 °C, the inland and mountain areas averaging 26 °C, and the higher mountain regions, 23 °C.

 

Best time to visit

The best time to travel throughout Indonesia is really between May and September, although rain needn’t spoil your trip; it many places it only falls for a couple of hours daily, with much of the country managing perfectly well whatever the weather. There are typically fewer tourists during the monsoon season, which is an added benefit. It really depends how far off the track you intend to travel, since muddy back roads can be a problem, and rough seas can render some islands inaccessible to smaller boats.
